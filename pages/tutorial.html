Jumping Ape

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or 'A' and 'D' to move the monkey left and right. To win the game you need to go through all levels. In each level, the goal is to reach finish platform by jumping up. If you fall down, you will die. Watch out some platforms have special features like disappearing.
